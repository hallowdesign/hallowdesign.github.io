Hey There,

I'm Kelsey, an Australian Graphic Designer dwelling about in Adelaide. Since I started Middle school long ago, I have always had an intense passion for Design done right. Design that is staright-forward, practical, but not at all boring. Design to me, defines as a functional layout that still interests and pleases the viewer.

I started HALLOW Design Group out of a passion and frustration. While design always needs to be functional, many modern designs are lifeless and flat in hues. Minimalism is great and all, but having the flare and flamboyance is what makes design interesting and exciting!

Which is where I establish a lot of my design aspects from, direct and interesting in design. Take Mello, my Android Icon Pack for example. The icons are all centerted around the same circular shape. The details of the icon are always at the center of the background, making it a very direct viewing experience. That doesn't stop the icons from being boring, however. Each and every icon has also been designed to Pop! off your screen, making the viewing extremely delightful, whenever you unlock your device.
